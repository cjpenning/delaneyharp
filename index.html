Repertoire

Here are some of my harp music options.

Classical

Classical selections, particularly the Baroque, are well suited for the harp.

  • Canon in D (Pachelbel)
  • Arioso (Bach)
  • Air on a G String (Bach)
  • Prelude (Bach)
  • Spring (Vivaldi)
  • Jesu Joy of Man’s Desiring (Bach)

Celtic

The harp is a traditional instrument of Ireland and Wales. Many beautiful songs have been written for the harp.

  • My Love is Like Red Red Rose
  • Sally Gardens / The Maids of Morne Shore
  • Eleanor Plunkett
  • Blind Mary

Pop/Contemporary

Many contemporary or pop songs translate well to the harp.

  • What a Wonderful World
  • Over the Rainbow
  • A River Flows in You / Bella’s Theme
  • Memory (Cats)
  • Moon River
  • Unchained Melody
